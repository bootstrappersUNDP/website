Contact

The website is developed by Aleksandar and Tica. Have questions or suggestions? Contact us here.

Aleksandar Bauranov is a civil engineer and doctoral student at the Harvard Graduate School of Design where he explores the relationship between cities, mobility and autonomous vehicles. Aleksandar was a researcher at NEXTOR, an aviation research institute, where he worked on projects led by the FAA and NASA on the modernization and automation of the National Airspace System. His other recent projects tackled a variety of interdisciplinary topics in transportation, economics and operations research. Aleksandar holds a master’s degree in Transportation Engineering from the University of California Berkeley, and a bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Belgrade.

Tica Lin is a PhD Student in Visual Computing Group at Harvard University, exploring Immersive Analytics and Human-Computer Interaction. Prior to embarking on the PhD journey, she was a Data Visualization Designer at Visa, and UX Developer at Philadelphia 76ers. Tica received M.S. in Digital Media from Georgia Tech and B.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University. Her research interests lie in exploring novel visualization and interaction with immersive technologies, in particular the blending of physical and digital world.
